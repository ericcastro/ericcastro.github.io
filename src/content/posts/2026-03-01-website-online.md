Every time I thought of putting together some sort of personal landing page for myself under my `cast.ro` domain, something made it fail miserably. Most of the time it was my laziness, because let's be honest: motivating yourself to keep a personal website updated is quite the challenge when the only people interested are maybe some colleague aside from your mom and your dog.

And part of that laziness came from never fully adopting the static website approach, plus never being able to come up with some cool design that made my website truly reflect who I am at the moment.

Then ChatGPT became a thing, and as a subscriber I gave Codex a try for the first time. This is what resulted from it.

I like the irony of myself constantly struggling against retro computing nostalgia and yet using AI to build a website that looks and feels like Windows 95. Now that surely reflects who I am at the moment.

Pretty satisfied.
