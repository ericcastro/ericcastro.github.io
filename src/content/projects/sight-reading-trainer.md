This project was built to make sight-reading practice less repetitive and less vulnerable to muscle-memory cheating.

Instead of drilling the same exercises over and over, it generates fresh sheet music on demand and lets you play it on a MIDI keyboard with immediate feedback.

- Randomly creates new musical sheet music for sight-reading practice
- Uses a MIDI keyboard for real-time input
- Shows timing feedback directly on screen
- Generates a new exercise every time you hit `Next`

The whole point was to keep practice honest by making memorization much harder than actual reading.
