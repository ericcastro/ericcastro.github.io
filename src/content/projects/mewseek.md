Formerly known as *iSlsk*, MewSeek was a music streaming and MP3 download app for jailbroken iPhones and iPads.

It was built to be quick, simple, and very much of its time: a jailbreak-era utility that spread through forums, blogs, YouTube reviews, and word of mouth.

- Quick and simple music streaming and MP3 downloads
- Popular in the iOS jailbreak community
- Reached roughly 20K daily active users at its peak between 2009 and 2011
- Featured on Wired and across many tech blogs and YouTube reviews in multiple languages

It remains one of the clearest examples of the kind of software that could only really exist in that specific moment of mobile computing.
